CUSTOM LIGHTING SOLUTIONS

Working with a variety of renowned manufacturers, we are able to offer state-of-the-art, innovative fixtures tailored to be an integral and unobtrusive part of your space. We specialize in bringing your vision to life using custom products and collaborative efforts geared to bring conceptual ideas to reality. Our team is fluent in Revit, 3D modeling, Bluebeam and Photometric studies in AGI.

LIGHTING DESIGN

With 27 years of industry experience in a broad range of lighting design applications including worldwide retail stores, gas stations, residential properties, hotels and restaurants, we work at the forefront of lighting technology. Our team is dedicated to creating maximum impact with creative lighting that integrates harmoniously into its environment.

ARCHITECTURAL & DESIGN SOFTWARE

The ISP Team works with many software platforms from AutoCAD, Revit, 3D sketch-up, AGI, and Photoshop. These software tools allow the team to work with architects on presentations, conceptual design concepts and through the design process of the project. At ISP we pride ourselves in having the latest versions of the software that allow our team to stay current and up to date. The team is constantly learning and we are constantly doing training to better our services for our clientele and the tools we use to provide our service.
