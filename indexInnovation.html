GRID SYSTEM

In 2017 ISP Design and Price Industries partnered up to design and manufacture a complete modular ceiling system with its own control system. GRID Modular Ceiling System features interlocking components for quick and easy installation and reconfiguration to accommodate evolving space requirements. Integration of building systems including lighting, audio, and security allows for single source supply, reducing coordination, minimizing installation time and ensuring compatibility of all components as well as visual consistency throughout.

GRID SYSTEM

GRID Modular Ceiling System is a unique building system and control solution that features interlocking two and eight feet modules, and four inch connectors for simple, efficient installation without the need for specialized tools or equipment.

< swipe >
GRID SYSTEM

A variety of control systems including lighting, audio, sensors, and security are available as GRID modules. All integrated devices are low voltage and feature quick-connect ports for use with the connectors. Connectors are suspended from the ceiling by a cable or threaded rod and may enclose an electrical multiport box that can supply power and communications to up to four devices.

RICHTER
& ISP DESIGN COLLECTION
< swipe >

After developing the mono-point we moved into a sleek but minimal pendant fixture. The uniqueness of this fixture was the light guide which allowed for this even distribution of light that provides no glare on the work surface with a very thin profile. The four-foot fixture is ideal for open office plans as well as closed offices. After creating these two amazing luminaires we moved into making a downlight family that was perfect for the Modular and Prefab Market. The new downlight family has a reduced and beautiful trim and simply cuts itself in a completely finished ceiling - providing a perfect edge detail. These fixtures allowed for a simple collar to hold the downlight in place while providing a small two-inch aperture with a lumen package of 1400 providing this amazing quality of light. We added a Flash Mate® jumper which allowed it to be a family of quick connect systems.
